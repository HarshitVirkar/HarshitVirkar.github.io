About US

The Student Union Council is the prime student body of the Ramrao Adik Institute Technology and is responsible for all the cultural and social events that take place in the college.

Over the years the SUC has been working in inculcating teamspirit, coordination and leadership qualities in the students of the college.

The Student Union Council is FOR THE STUDENTS, OF THE STUDENTS AND BY THE STUDENTS

EVENTS that we Organize

FE ORIENTATION

Not the first day of your college.
It's the first day of a journey,
The best journey of your life.

FRESHER'S DAY

This is the most awaited day by not just the first years, but also by the seniors. A true RAITian would understand the meaning behind it.

T-SHIRT LAUNCH

It's no ordinary T shirt.
It's the symbol of our unity
We at RAIT, wear it with pride and dignity.

GANESH FESTIVAL

Every year RAIT celebrates the Ganesh festival with ecstacy and radiance. The Aagaman and Visarjan are filled with energy dance and music. Also don't miss out on the Satya Narayan puja, Mahaprasad and the Blood Donation drive during the festival

TEACHER'S DAY

Our teachers selflessly devote their time in educating us, so on Teacher's Day, we make sure that we do our best in showing them how thankful we are. It's celebrated pompously, well that's just the RAIT way.

HORIZON

It's not just a college fest.
It's a feeling.
It's an emotion.

TIE SAREE DAY

This is not as simple as it's name, but we like to call it the Rose Day. Well, there needs no introduction for a Rose Day. So let your hearts out and have fun.

TRADITIONAL DAY

The Even semester seems so boring after Horizon, but wait, that's not all. Traditional Day adds life to our monotonous college life and also to show our love for the Indian culture.

BE FAREWELL

We have already said more goodbyes than are necessary. Those were goodbyes that brought about the end of partings. We taught each other that no parting is possible.
